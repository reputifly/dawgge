Terms & Conditions

Please review our policies to ensure a safe and happy environment for all.

1. Enrollment & Assessment

All dogs must undergo a temperament assessment before enrollment. We reserve the right to refuse entry to any dog that displays aggressive behavior or signs of illness.

2. Health & Vaccinations

To ensure the safety of our pack, all dogs must have up-to-date vaccinations (Distemper, Parvovirus, Hepatitis) and be on regular flea/tick prevention.

  • Vaccination records must be provided upon registration.
  • Sick dogs or those with contagious conditions will not be admitted.

3. Cancellations & Packages

Package validity periods are strictly enforced. Cancellations for daycare or transport must be made at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a deduction from your package.

4. Revision Policy

Dawggedytails reserves the right to revise these terms and conditions at any time. Significant changes will be communicated to enrolled parents.

5. Liability

While we take every precaution to ensure the safety of your dog, Dawggedytails is not liable for any injuries, illnesses, or accidents that may occur during play or transport, unless due to proven negligence.
